Mahdi Farsi

CFD · LES & Turbulence · Experimental Fluid Mechanics · Electronics Cooling

I am a PhD-trained mechanical engineer specializing in computational fluid dynamics (CFD), turbulence modeling, experimental fluid mechanics, heat transfer, and thermal management of electronic systems. My current postdoctoral work focuses on wind tunnel experiments, Particle Image Velocimetry (PIV), active-grid turbulence characterization, hot-wire measurements, and automated data acquisition.

My work combines high-fidelity numerical simulation, laboratory experimentation, and custom data analysis using Python, MATLAB, and Fortran. I have applied these tools to turbulent transport, pollutant source localization, wind-energy wake measurements, industrial CFD, and electronics thermal management.
